 Notes
________________________________________________________________
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
> The 'Ch', 'Cf', and 'Cb' filters disable the character, foreground color, and
  background color respectively from being drawn.

> The 'Specific' tool option specifies whether characters that look identical
  will also be overwritten.

> The 'Import Image' function allows you to turn a file into a visual
  representation of its data.

> Transparent and invert colors will always overwrite. These colors are only
  needed for images used in programs that require transparency.

> The undo feature will lag the program when larger images are involved.

> The 'Attributes' image format allows images to have transparency and invertion colors.
